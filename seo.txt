I checked the site. Since this is a **business portfolio/agency website**, your SEO strategy should be completely different from CatalStudio or Nivasetu.

Your goal is:

```text
Google Ranking
↓
Service Pages
↓
Case Studies
↓
Blog Content
↓
Lead Generation
↓
Client Calls
```

Most agency websites make the mistake of having:

```text
Home
About
Services
Contact
```

and then wonder why Google sends no traffic.

---

# What You Need To Build

## Main Pages

```text
/
about
contact
portfolio
case-studies
blog
pricing
careers
```

---

## Service Pages (Most Important)

Each service gets its own page.

Example:

```text
/services/web-development

/services/mobile-app-development

/services/flutter-development

/services/ui-ux-design

/services/ecommerce-development

/services/seo-services

/services/digital-marketing

/services/custom-software-development

/services/cloud-consulting

/services/ai-development
```

Each page should target its own keyword.

---

## Location Pages

Huge SEO opportunity.

Example:

```text
/web-development-company-in-surat

/mobile-app-development-company-in-ahmedabad

/software-development-company-in-gujarat

/flutter-app-development-company-in-india

/website-development-company-in-india
```

Google loves these when done correctly.

---
